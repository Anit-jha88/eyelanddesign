Single Vision Lenses

Comfort: They are more comfortable and easier to wear than spectacles.

Freedom: Contact lenses give you complete freedom in movement and allow you to enjoy clear vision even during physical or sporting activity.

Peripheral vision: Since the contact lenses are worn directly over the cornea, they provide a natural field of vision.

No fogging: With contact lenses, there is no fogging as you step out of air conditioned environment.

Wearing sunglasses: Enjoy the option of wearing sunglasses when they want to.

Natural look: Choose contact lenses over spectacles for a combination of increased comfort and superior vision while you continue to look just the way you are!

Monthly contact lenses: These lenses can be worn for a period of 30 days. They can be worn for 10 – 12 hrs at a stretch. They should be cleaned twice, before and after wear and disinfected as prescribed. They are more comfortable than yearly lenses as they have 55% water content and better oxygen permeability and also have better hygiene levels.

Monthly disposable lenses are available in two options:

Monthly Disposable Lens (Spherical)

Monthly Disposable Lens (Toric)

Daily contact lenses: These lenses can be worn for a period of 12 hours at a stretch. This is the best option for those who seek highest levels of hygiene. Also, the hassle of maintaining the lenses is eliminated in the usage of Daily Contact lenses. Daily disposable contact lenses offer much better oxygen permeability leading to more comfort.

Daily disposable lenses are available in three options:

Daily Disposable Lens (Spherical)

Daily Disposable Lens (Toric)
